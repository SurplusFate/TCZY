    </div>
  </div>

<div style="background:rgba(102,126,234,0.08); padding:16px; border-radius:12px; margin-bottom:16px; border-left:4px solid #667eea;">

</div>

## 🔍 四、如何选择与接受工作：机缘巧合与现实妥协

    </ul>
  </div>

    <ul style="margin:0; padding-left:20px;">
