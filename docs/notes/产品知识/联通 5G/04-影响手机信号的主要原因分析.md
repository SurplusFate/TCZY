## 一、手机终端（硬件与设计）

<div style="background:rgba(102,126,234,0.06);border:1px solid rgba(102,126,234,0.15);border-radius:12px;padding:24px 28px;margin-bottom:28px;">

<div style="background:rgba(245,158,11,0.06);padding:12px 16px;border-radius:6px;font-size:14px;color:#666;border:1px solid rgba(245,158,11,0.15);">
